Concept statement

Motor vehicle crashes are a leading cause of death among those aged 1 to 54 in the U.S. This is why I wanted to create a site to raise awareness about one of the safest choices drivers and passengers can make.

We live in a global society, where the number of cars will double in the near future. It is important to promote awareness and provide accessible information for everyone about how to protect their loved ones inside their motor vehicles.

Design choices

I wanted to create a clean page with clear information about my topic. The white spaces are designed to improve readability and guide the userâ€™s attention through the page.

The images and color selection are designed to create a visual balance in every section. For example, on page 2, the top left cyan block is compensated with the image on the bottom right, which has a large amount of black color.

I selected Source Pro typography, both variants Serif, and Sans serif because I wanted to have consistency in the 2 fonts selection and a nice blend between the h2 and the h3 elements.

Don't make me think principles

  • Take advantage of conventions.

    With the use of images that are related to traffic signs icons.

  • Create effective visual hierarchies.

    Using a big header, titles and borders that help users identify each section.

  • Break pages up into clearly defined areas.

    With the use of margins and white space.
